Chef's Table

An intimate restaurant dinner with a few couples or a small group. Enjoy custom-paired courses that you won’t otherwise find on the menu. Meet the chef and learn about what inspired your meal.

Secret Supper

Multiple courses and glasses with Northern Colorado’s best chefs and bartenders in a surprise location. You will be joined by 30-40 other food fanatics at unique places such as coffee roasters, farms and breweries.

Pop-Up Parties

Be one of the first to experience some of Fort Collins’ best new spaces, while eating great food catered by local food trucks.

Classes + Tastings

Whether you’re learning how to best photograph the dish in front of you or what merlot pairs best with your evening menu, you’ll come as a novice, and leave as an expert.

Supper Club

Talented cooks, dinner party aficionados and adventure lovers unite to tour the town. Get an opportunity to tour the town’s underground food culture via down-home cooking by amateur Fort Collins chefs.

First Bites

Be the first to try new restaurant spreads. Whether it’s a seasonal menu change at your favorite spot, or a brand new chef or eatery, you’ll know where to send your neighbors and friends.
