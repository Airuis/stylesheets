Pricing starts at $999.00 per person

Arizona is set to play host to yet another major sports spectacle when the second College Football Playoff National Championship kicks off at University of Phoenix Stadium. The top four teams competing in the playoff include No. 1 ranked Clemson versus No. 4 Oklahoma in the Orange Bowl, and No. 2 Alabama versus No. 3 Michigan State in the Cotton Bowl. The winners of the semifinals will go on to face each other in Glendale for a place in college football immortality.
